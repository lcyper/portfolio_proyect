Sefaria - daily study

What I did:

It is a mobile app where you can follow to study a special section for that day from different books, it is geared for daily study for Orthodox Jews.

How it works:

You have on the main screen different links to choose from, inside you will have the content with various settings available, you can also mark if you completed it and a section dedicated to progress, it is a pretty complete application, I hope you enjoy it!
